1. Is the hot water ON and BRU light ON?

Ask the resident to check that the hot water is ON at the thermostat and that the red light above the water droplet on the BRU is ON.

2. Is a timer incorrectly set?

If a timer has been set up, Switchee may need to adjust the hot water timings. Contact Switchee if necessary.

3. Are there any boiler error codes?

Ask the resident to look for error codes like F1, F22. If present, arrange for boiler servicing.

4. Reset Switchee and boiler system

Advise power cycling the thermostat and boiler isolator switch to reset the system.
