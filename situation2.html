Can the resident select hot water ON at thermostat? Is BRU light above hot water symbol ON? Is the hot water tap getting warm? Use red override button to control hot water Reset Switchee system if necessary Organize housing association repair visit if still unresolved

1. Can the resident select hot water ON at thermostat?

Ask the resident to press the hot water symbol on the Switchee thermostat and confirm 'Hot Water ON' appears.

2. Is BRU light above hot water symbol ON?

Check if the red light above the water droplet symbol on the BRU is illuminated, indicating communication is OK.

3. Is the hot water tap getting warm?

If yes, Switchee system is working correctly. If not, continue troubleshooting.

4. Use red override button to control hot water

If the thermostat is not controlling the hot water, ask the resident to use the red override button on the BRU.

5. Reset Switchee system if necessary

Advise a reset: turn off at the mains or boiler isolator for a minute, then turn back on.

6. Organize housing association repair visit if still unresolved

Book engineer if issues persist after reset, or if override button doesn't work.
